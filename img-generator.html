Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI Avatar Generator

How does the AI avatar generator work?

Our AI avatar generator uses advanced machine learning models to interpret your text prompts and generate corresponding avatar videos. You simply describe what you want, and our system creates a unique avatar video based on your description.

What video formats are supported?

We support MP4 format for all downloads, which is compatible with all major social media platforms. You can download in vertical (9:16) format for Reels/Shorts, square (1:1) format, or landscape (16:9) format.

Can I customize the avatar after generation?

Yes! After generating your avatar, you can make adjustments to the prompt and regenerate, or use our premium editing tools to fine-tune specific aspects of your avatar.

Is there a limit to how many avatars I can create?

Free users can generate up to 5 avatars per day. Our premium plans offer unlimited generations and additional features.

How do I download my avatar videos?

After your avatar is generated, click on any of the download buttons to save your video in the selected format. The video will download directly to your device.
